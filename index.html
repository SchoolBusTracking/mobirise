School Bus Tracking

It's an application that provides services for three users: parents, school administration, and school bus drivers.The main purpose of the application is to ensure the safety of the children in school buses and reduce the incidents of forgotten them.

  •  Main Functions:
  • Contact with us:

Live Tracking

allows parents and the school administration to track the school bus.

Notification

send notifications and SMS to parents and the school administration in specific situations.

Estimating Time

Show the expected time of child home arrival on the map.

attendance List

list of all the children who will attending the school will be sent to the drivers every school day.
